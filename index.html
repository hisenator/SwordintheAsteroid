Sword in the Asteroid

In the far future, Earth has joined the great many civilizations of the universe, and has achieved the final frontier. On this desolate moon, in the outerspace highway, is a pit stop where many ships rest and refuel. Here the starliner the Infinity hangs in the view of pink Crystal Moon. We are aboard the ship of an adventurer and explorer, searching for a sword lodged in the ground.







Explore the Year 2455's Final Frontier

Artist Statement

This 3D scene is inspired by my space opera rom-com idea I had when I was younger.

I wanted to create a scene that lets you see the place within it, in a space that is explorable. While I wanted it to be a "fly through space" sort of feel, I couldnt get it too look as good as invisioned.

Using the demo floor I instead made this asterorid type surface, and decided to add the story idea's namesake to the scene. I created the look by messing with the colors of the lights, background and adjusting the gravity of the movement and jumping.
